About Us

"Crafting unforgettable moments through flavor and design."

Established in Uzanbazar, Aira Cafe is born from a desire to combine high-end architecture with exceptional culinary craftsmanship. We source our coffee beans from award-winning single estates in the misty hills of Chikmagalur, carefully roasting them to reveal notes of rich cocoa, citrus, and hazelnut.

Our kitchen specializes in house-fermented sourdough plates, French pastries baked fresh every morning, and comfort dishes reinvented with modern culinary techniques. Whether you are seeking a quiet morning workspace or an intimate dinner with friends, Aira offers a sensory escape away from the city's bustle.
